The Journey Above the Clouds

Welcome to the Tipari Madan Negi Ropeway. This isn't just a ride; it's a gateway to the glorious history of Tehri and a tribute to the villages that sacrificed for the Dam.

Spanning approximately 1 KM across the vast Tehri Lake, this 10-minute journey offers breathtaking views of the Dam, the Himalayan ranges, and the submerged history of the old city.

Enjoy a comfortable cabin ride, click amazing photos, and savor brunch with a scenic view at our station.
